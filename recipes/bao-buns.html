Bao buns

There are endless variations on the steamed bun across different cultures and regions: different dough recipes, different fillings, different cooking methods.

Ingredients

  • Bao bun dough mix
  • Cabbage
  • Onion
  • Five spice
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds to garnish(optional)

Steps

  1. Roll the dough out
  2. Cut the vegetables
  3. Mix the vegetables
  4. stuff the dumplings
  5. Cook them in a steamer
  6. Serve with soy sauce to dip!
  7. Enjoy!
