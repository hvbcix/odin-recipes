Stir fry

Stir frying is a Chinese cooking technique in which ingredients are fried in a small amount of very hot oil while being stirred in a wok.

I like to stir fry the vegetables that take a little longer to cook first. Like the carrots, mushrooms and baby corn. Then I add in the remainder ingredients. Broccoli, snow peas and the bell peppers do not take as long to cook.

Ingredients

  • Pack of instant noodles
  • Chicken or tofu
  • Carrots
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables
  2. Cut the protein of choice into chunks
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Pour oil into the wok
  5. Heat up the wok on high heat
  6. Fry the protein
  7. Set the protein aside
  8. Fry the vegetables
  9. Put the meat back to the wok with the noodles
  10. Add soy sauce
  11. Plate and add garnish (scallions, peanuts, lemon)
  12. Enjoy!
