Mochi

Homemade Mochi Ice Cream are Japanese rice cakes with an ice cream center in a soft, slightly chewy, sweet rice dough. You wonâ€™t believe how easy it is to make yourself!

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Sugar
  • Ice cream
  • Water

Steps

  1. Roll the dough
  2. Stuff it with ice cream
  3. Freeze overnight
  4. Take out from the freezer 15 minutes before serving.
  5. Enjoy!
